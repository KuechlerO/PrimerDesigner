Allele-specific PCR (AS-PCR / ARMS)

This mode designs two allele-specific reactions: WT-specific and MUT-specific. By default, primers are designed so that the allele-discriminating base is at the 3′ end of the allele-specific primer (no extra intentional mismatch).
